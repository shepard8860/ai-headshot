## 中文版

### 引言

AI职业照（以下简称"本应用"）由本应用开发团队运营。我们深知个人信息对您的重要性，并会尽全力保护您的个人信息安全。本隐私政策旨在帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用、存储、共享和保护您的个人信息，以及您享有的相关权利。

**请您在使用本应用前，仔细阅读并充分理解本隐私政策的全部内容。** 如您不同意本隐私政策的任何内容，您应立即停止使用本应用。一旦您开始使用本应用，即视为您已同意本隐私政策。

### 一、我们收集的信息

#### 1.1 您主动提供的信息

- **人脸照片/自拍**：当您使用本应用的证件照生成功能时，您需要上传或拍摄一张自拍照。这是本应用的核心功能所必需的信息。
- **购买记录**：当您通过 Apple App Store 进行应用内购买时，我们会收集您的交易凭证和购买记录，用于解锁相应的高清生成服务。

#### 1.2 我们自动收集的信息

- **设备标识符**：我们可能会收集您的设备匿名标识符（非广告标识符），用于统计分析、故障排查和改进服务质量。
- **应用使用数据**：包括功能使用频率、生成成功率、操作步骤等匿名统计数据，用于优化产品体验。

#### 1.3 本地处理的信息

- **人脸质量检测**：在您上传照片后，本应用会在设备本地使用 Apple Vision 框架进行人脸检测和质量评估（包括人脸角度、光线、遮挡检测等）。**该过程完全在本地完成，不会上传原始人脸数据。**

### 二、我们如何使用您的信息

我们严格遵守"最小必要"原则，仅在以下目的范围内使用您的信息：

1. **提供核心服务**：将您的人脸照片与职业照模板进行 AI 融合，生成职业证件照。
2. **人脸质量预检**：在本地检测设备拍摄的人脸质量，确保合成效果。
3. **订单处理与验证**：处理您的应用内购买订单，验证支付状态并解锁服务。
4. **服务优化**：基于匿名统计数据改进算法效果、修复问题和优化用户体验。
5. **安全保障**：防止欺诈、滥用和非法活动，保护账户和服务安全。

### 三、数据存储与传输

#### 3.1 数据上传与处理

- 您的人脸照片将上传至我们的云端服务器，通过商汤 SenseNova 和/或阿里云通义万相 API 进行 AI 图像生成处理。
- **数据存储地点**：中国大陆境内（阿里云 OSS、LeanCloud 国内节点）。
- **数据跨境传输**：本应用不涉及将您的个人信息传输至中国境外。**所有处理均在中国大陆境内完成。**

#### 3.2 数据保留期限

- **原始人脸照片**：在上传后 30 天内自动删除，仅在生成过程中临时存储。
- **生成的证件照**：根据您的意愿，可保存至您的本地相册。我们不在服务器长期存储生成的证件照。
- **订单记录**：根据《电子商务法》要求，保留至少 3 年。
- **匿名统计数据**：可保留更长时间用于产品改进。

### 四、信息共享与披露

我们不会向任何第三方出售您的个人信息。仅在以下情况下，我们可能会披露您的信息：

1. **服务提供商**：我们可能与云存储服务商（阿里云）、数据库服务商（LeanCloud）、AI 模型服务商（商汤科技）共享必要的数据，以提供核心服务。这些服务商均受严格的数据处理协议约束，且不得将您的信息用于其他目的。
2. **法律要求**：根据法律法规、法院命令或政府部门的合法要求，我们可能需要披露您的信息。
3. **权利保护**：为了保护本应用、用户或公众的合法权益，防止欺诈或安全问题时，我们可能在法律允许的范围内披露信息。

### 五、您的权利

根据中国《个人信息保护法》，您享有以下权利：

1. **知情权**：您有权了解我们如何处理您的个人信息（即本政策所述内容）。
2. **访问权与复制权**：您有权访问和复制我们持有的您的个人信息。
3. **更正权与补充权**：如您的个人信息不准确或不完整，您有权要求更正或补充。
4. **删除权**：在特定情况下，您有权要求我们删除您的个人信息。
5. **撤回同意权**：您可以随时撤回对我们处理您个人信息的同意。但请注意，撤回同意可能导致您无法使用本应用的核心功能。
6. **可携带权**：在技术可行的条件下，您有权要求将您的个人信息转移至您指定的个人信息处理者。

如需行使上述权利，请通过本政策末尾的联系方式与我们联系。我们将在 15 个工作日内响应您的请求。

### 六、未成年人保护

本应用主要面向年满 18 周岁的成年职场人士。**我们不会主动收集未成年人的个人信息。** 如果您是未满 14 周岁的未成年人，请勿使用本应用。如果我们发现意外收集了未成年人的个人信息，将立即删除。

### 七、信息安全

我们采用业界标准的安全措施保护您的个人信息：

- **传输加密**：所有数据通过 HTTPS/TLS 1.3 加密传输。
- **存储加密**：云端存储数据采用 AES-256 加密。
- **访问控制**：服务器实施严格的访问权限管理和操作审计。
- **安全审计**：定期进行安全漏洞扫描和风险评估。

尽管我们采取了合理的安全措施，但请理解**互联网传输不存在绝对的安全保障**。

### 八、算法与深度合成说明

本应用使用基于深度学习的生成式人工智能技术（AIGC）进行图像合成。

- **算法备案**：本应用所涉深度合成算法已按照中国《互联网信息服务深度合成管理规定》完成算法备案。
- **显著标识**：生成的 AI 合成图像不会添加显式水印，但您应知悉该图像为 AI 生成内容，建议在使用前向相关方（如用人单位）如实说明。
- **内容安全**：我们已建立内容审核机制，防止生成违法、违规或不良内容。

### 九、隐私政策的更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本应用内显著位置公布，并注明更新日期。**如我们对本政策进行重大变更，我们将在变更生效前通过应用内通知或弹窗方式告知您。**

## English Version

### Introduction

AI Headshot (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is operated by the App development team. We understand the importance of your personal information and will do our utmost to protect the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information, as well as the rights you have in relation to your information.

**Please read and fully understand all the contents of this Privacy Policy before using the App.** If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, you should immediately stop using the App. By using the App, you are deemed to have agreed to this Privacy Policy.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

- **Face Photos/Selfies**: When you use the App's headshot generation feature, you need to upload or take a selfie. This is essential information required for the core functionality of the App.
- **Purchase Records**: When you make in-app purchases through the Apple App Store, we collect your transaction receipts and purchase records to unlock corresponding HD generation services.

#### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- **Device Identifiers**: We may collect anonymous device identifiers (non-advertising identifiers) for statistical analysis, troubleshooting, and service quality improvement.
- **App Usage Data**: Includes anonymous statistics such as feature usage frequency, generation success rates, and operation steps, used to optimize the product experience.

#### 1.3 Information Processed Locally

- **Face Quality Detection**: After you upload a photo, the App uses the Apple Vision framework on your device to perform face detection and quality assessment (including face angle, lighting, and occlusion detection). **This process is completed entirely locally and does not upload raw face data.**

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We strictly adhere to the principle of "minimum necessity" and use your information only for the following purposes:

1. **Providing Core Services**: AI fusion of your face photo with professional headshot templates to generate career portrait photos.
2. **Face Quality Pre-check**: Locally detecting the quality of faces captured by the device to ensure synthesis quality.
3. **Order Processing and Verification**: Processing your in-app purchase orders, verifying payment status, and unlocking services.
4. **Service Optimization**: Improving algorithm performance, fixing issues, and optimizing user experience based on anonymous statistical data.
5. **Security Protection**: Preventing fraud, abuse, and illegal activities, and protecting account and service security.

### 3. Data Storage and Transmission

#### 3.1 Data Upload and Processing

- Your face photos will be uploaded to our cloud servers and processed through the SenseNova API (SenseTime) and/or Tongyi Wanxiang API (Alibaba Cloud) for AI image generation.
- **Data Storage Location**: Within mainland China (Alibaba Cloud OSS, LeanCloud domestic nodes).
- **Cross-border Data Transfer**: The App does not involve transferring your personal information outside of mainland China. **All processing is completed within mainland China.**

#### 3.2 Data Retention Period

- **Original Face Photos**: Automatically deleted within 30 days after upload; temporarily stored only during the generation process.
- **Generated Headshots**: According to your preference, can be saved to your local photo album. We do not long-term store generated headshots on our servers.
- **Order Records**: Retained for at least 3 years as required by the E-Commerce Law.
- **Anonymous Statistics**: May be retained for longer periods for product improvement.

### 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to any third parties. We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**: We may share necessary data with cloud storage providers (Alibaba Cloud), database service providers (LeanCloud), and AI model service providers (SenseTime) to provide core services. These service providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and may not use your information for other purposes.
2. **Legal Requirements**: We may be required to disclose your information in accordance with laws, regulations, court orders, or legitimate requests from government authorities.
3. **Rights Protection**: To protect the legitimate rights and interests of the App, users, or the public, and to prevent fraud or security issues, we may disclose information to the extent permitted by law.

### 5. Your Rights

In accordance with China's Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL), you have the following rights:

1. **Right to Know**: You have the right to understand how we process your personal information (as described in this Policy).
2. **Right to Access and Copy**: You have the right to access and obtain copies of your personal information that we hold.
3. **Right to Correct and Supplement**: If your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request correction or supplementation.
4. **Right to Deletion**: Under specific circumstances, you have the right to request that we delete your personal information.
5. **Right to Withdraw Consent**: You may withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal information at any time. Please note that withdrawing consent may result in your inability to use the core features of the App.
6. **Right to Data Portability**: Where technically feasible, you have the right to request that your personal information be transferred to a personal information processor designated by you.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information at the end of this Policy. We will respond to your requests within 15 working days.

### 6. Protection of Minors

This App is primarily intended for adult working professionals aged 18 and above. **We do not actively collect personal information from minors.** If you are a minor under the age of 14, please do not use this App. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

### 7. Information Security

We adopt industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

- **Transmission Encryption**: All data is transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encryption.
- **Storage Encryption**: Cloud-stored data is encrypted using AES-256.
- **Access Control**: Servers implement strict access permission management and operation auditing.
- **Security Audits**: Regular security vulnerability scanning and risk assessments are conducted.

Although we have taken reasonable security measures, please understand that **there is no absolute security guarantee for Internet transmission**.

### 8. Algorithm and Deep Synthesis Statement

This App uses generative artificial intelligence technology (AIGC) based on deep learning for image synthesis.

- **Algorithm Filing**: The deep synthesis algorithms used in this App have been filed in accordance with China's "Provisions on the Administration of Deep Synthesis Internet Information Services."
- **Identification**: Generated AI-synthesized images will not carry explicit watermarks, but you should be aware that these images are AI-generated content. We recommend that you inform relevant parties (such as employers) truthfully before use.
- **Content Safety**: We have established content moderation mechanisms to prevent the generation of illegal, non-compliant, or harmful content.
